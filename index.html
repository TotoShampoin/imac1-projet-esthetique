How Strong Is This AI?

What is this?

This is an application that calls the ML5 image classifier MobileNet to analyze a provided image. The goal is to see how well it performs, even when modifications are brought to the image.

You can use this default blue jay, or provide your own picture, or even use your phone's camera. You can also use the various settings to change the image itself, and you can even draw on the image using your finger.

Have fun with the settings, see how it affects the classification, and don't be afraid to confuse the AI! :D
